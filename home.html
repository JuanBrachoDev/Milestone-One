Who are we?

We are pleased to announce the 10th The Adventure Begins Winter Festival, brought to you by the expert hands of our dedicated team to bring entertainment and joy to everyone in Dublin.

Fit for all ages, this event is sure to bring you a warm smile in this chilly season, whether you are in to enjoy the fun activities we have prepared, or would rather join our team of organizers.

We look forward to seeing you in any of the locations!

Join us!

  • Get a stand spot in all days of our festival, sell your merchandise!
  • Guaranteed parking spot just next to the event.
  • Promotion for your brand(s) in our social media accounts.

Activities

Aurora Borealis Sightseeing

-This activity is only available on 7/12-

The National Weather Organization of Ireland has forecast visible and bright lights in early December, come and join us in this lovely off-road location and participate in all of the other fun activities while enjoying the starry night sky nature offers.

Ice Skating Ballet

Come and join us in the Icicle Ballroom, for newbies and experts alike there is fun to be had, featuring classic songs for everyone to enjoy! We provide free skate rental for all attendants.

Food Faire

Feeling peckish? We have a selection of worldwide treats for any foodies out there, enjoy your favourite food with your favourite people!
